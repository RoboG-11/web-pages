Shingeki No Kyojin

The story of Shingeki no Kyojin takes us to a time when humanity has been on the verge of extinction due to the existence of titans. Giant beings apparently without intelligence whose goal is none other than to devour humans even though they do not need them to survive. The few survivors have built three walls higher than any titan, where they live peacefully without fear of being devoured. Humanity has lived in peace for over 100 years until the year 845, when a titan taller than the walls built for protection appears out of nowhere and creates a breach in one of the walls allowing all the titans to pass through. At that moment, humanity remembers that it lives in fear of being devoured by the titans.

Kimetsu No Yaiba

Since the death of his father, the responsibility of caring for the family falls on Tanjiro Kamado. Although they live in poverty on a mountain, the Kamado family enjoys a peaceful and happy life. One day, Tanjiro decides to go to the nearest village to sell charcoal. When he returns it is already dark and Tanjiro is forced to stay at the house of an acquaintance who warns him of the existence of demons that come out at night to devour humans. The next day, he finds a horrible situation. His family had been killed, except for one of his sisters, who had been turned into a demon. At that moment, Tanjiro decides to avenge his family and find a way to make his sister human again, but to do so he must become a demon slayer.

Brand New Animal

During the 21st century, the existence of anthropomorphic animals is discovered. Michiru lives as a normal human, until one day she transforms into a tanuki-human. She flees seeking refuge in an area of the city called "Anima City", created 10 years ago so that "animal-humans" can live in peace. There Michiru meets Shirou, a wolf-human who hates humans. Through Shirou, Michiru will learn about what life is like for anthropomorphic animals in the sanctuary city, until they become involved in a large-scale incident.

Spy x Family

SPY x FAMILY places us in a world at war between the countries of Westalis and Ostania (inspired by West and East Germany during the Cold War), where both countries deploy their best intelligence and espionage tools. Among all the spies, Twilight, Westalis' best agent, stands out, and this time he will have an important mission: to take care of Ostania's powerful politician, Donovan Desmond. Unfortunately, Desmond is paranoid about security and the only public appearances he makes are at his son's school functions, at the elitist Eden School. Because of this, Twilight's only way to get close to Donovan is to pretend to be a parent.

Hunter x Hunter

The plot places us in a fantastic world where there are evil beasts, hidden treasures... and hunters, people who live incredible adventures to unravel the mysteries of the planet. Gon Freecss is a young man who takes the hunter exams in order to obtain his license card. To pass them, he must prove that he has the skills required for such a complicated and dangerous job. In the tests he befriends three other candidates: the cheerful Leorio, always dressed in a suit and sunglasses; Kurapika, a rather serious blond boy with a strange look; and Killua, a real passive. From that moment on they become inseparable and begin to deal with both evil beasts and evil humans.

NHK ni Yōkoso!

Tatsuhiro Satou, a 22-year-old former college student entering his 4th year of unemployment. He leads a totally reclusive life as a hikikomori, where he finally comes to the bizarre conclusion that what is happening to him has to do with a conspiracy. One day when nothing in his life seems to change, he meets Misaki Nakahara, a mysterious girl who wants to try to rescue Tatsuhiro from his ostracism.

The Tatami Galaxy

The story begins with an unnamed protagonist, who starts his first year in college. There, he joins the tennis club to try to find love and have a rosy college life, but instead he meets Ozu, a troubled boy whom people mistake for a demon; and Akashi, a girl who usually responds coldly and dismissively to anyone who proposes a romantic date. After spending two years spoiling other people's love affairs with Ozu's help, during a festival, an angry mob sets out to end the lives of our protagonist and his companion, and after falling into the river he thinks "What would have happened if I had chosen a different club?"

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners

The story of Cyberpunk: Edgerunners takes place in the American city of Night City, a megacity in the Free State of Northern California. The city suffers from a great deal of corruption, cybernetic addiction and gang violence. The city is divided into six districts, each of which has its own precise living requirements, and is controlled by several different mega-corporations, including Arasaka Corporation and its rival Militech Corporation. The anime's story takes place primarily in Santo Domingo, Night City's oldest, poorest and most industrial district.

Neon Genesis Evangelion

The series opens in the year 2015 CE, fifteen years after the first disastrous contact with mysterious beings known as angels, which resulted in a worldwide cataclysm called Second Impact that halved the human population on Earth.21 To prevent future angel attacks, the UN established the NERV organization in Tokyo-3, which developed a series of biomechanical giants called Evangelion-abbreviated EVA-to combat them. Because the Evangelion can only be piloted by teenagers,2223 NERV's leader, Gendō Ikari, contacts his estranged son, Shinji, to pilot Evangelion Unit 01, and wipe out the Third Angel, which is attacking the city.
